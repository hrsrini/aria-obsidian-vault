### Interagency Guideline for establishing Safety & Soundness Standards
**Objective:** requires each federal banking agency (collectively, the agencies) to establish certain safety-and-soundness standards by regulation or by guideline for all insured depository institutions. Under section 39, the agencies must establish three types of standards: (1) operational and managerial standards; (2) compensation standards; and (3) such standards relating to asset quality, earnings, and stock valuation as they determine to be appropriate.

**Applies to:** All banks

**Roles:** Board, CRO
