## Summary

US Federal Banking Regulations covering **Operational** risk. This note covers 3 regulations with their objectives, bank size applicability, and role responsibilities.

## Regulations

### Basel Committee as Supervisory Banking (BCBS) Principles for the Sound
Management of
Operational Risk
**Objective:** describes eleven principles of sound operational risk management. These principles cover three main areas: (i) governance; (ii) the risk management environment; and (iii) the role of disclosure.

**Roles:** Board, CRO

### Sarbanes-Oxley Act (SOX) Section 404
**Objective:** Require management and auditor assessment of internal controls over financial reporting.

**Applies to:** Publicly traded banks

**Roles:** Board, CRO

### Interagency Guidelines Establishing Standards for Safety and Soundness
**Objective:** Establish operational, managerial, and internal control standards for banks.

**Applies to:** All banks

**Roles:** Board, CRO
