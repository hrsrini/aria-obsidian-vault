## Summary

US Federal Banking Regulations covering **Model** risk. This note covers 2 regulations with their objectives, bank size applicability, and role responsibilities.

## Regulations

### SR 11-7 Guidance on Model Risk Management
**Objective:** Set expectations for banks to manage risks associated with financial models. Requires comprehensive governance over model development, implementation, and validation, including board and senior management oversight.

**Applies to:** All banks; heightened standards for large, complex institutions

**Roles:** Board, CRO

### Basel III / US Capital Rules (model validation)
**Objective:** Require validation and oversight of models used for capital calculations.

**Applies to:** All banks using advanced approaches

**Roles:** Board, CRO, CTO, CISO
