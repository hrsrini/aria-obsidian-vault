## Summary

US Federal Banking Regulations covering **Credit** risk. This note covers 3 regulations with their objectives, bank size applicability, and role responsibilities.

## Regulations

### Basel III / US Capital Rules (12 CFR 3, 217, 324)
**Objective:** Ensure banks hold sufficient capital to absorb credit losses and promote stability.

**Applies to:** All banks; stricter standards for banks with assets > $250 billion or significant international activity

**Roles:** CRO

### FDIC Part 365 Real Estate Lending Standards
**Objective:** Set prudent standards for real estate lending and risk management.

**Applies to:** All FDIC-insured banks

**Roles:** CRO

### Interagency Guidelines for Loan Review Systems
**Objective:** Ensure independent and effective loan review to manage credit risk.

**Applies to:** All banks

**Roles:** CRO
